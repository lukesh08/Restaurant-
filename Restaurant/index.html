About Us

Welcome to Spice Route, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Established with a passion for bringing authentic flavors from around the world, we have been serving our community with love and dedication for years. Our journey began with a simple vision - to create a dining experience that celebrates the rich diversity of global cuisines while maintaining the highest standards of quality and taste. From traditional Indian delicacies to classic Italian favorites and innovative fusion creations, every dish at Spice Route tells a story of tradition, innovation, and culinary artistry. We believe that food is not just sustenance, but an experience that brings people together, creates memories, and celebrates life's special moments.

Lukesh - Owner & Founder

With over 15 years of experience in the culinary industry, Lukesh brings his passion for food and hospitality to every aspect of Spice Route.
